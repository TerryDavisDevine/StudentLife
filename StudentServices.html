Student Services

Information about other pages

Mental Health Support

We are here to support you with anything you are strugging with. Our drop-in services are available to all students, both on campus and online for students to come and share their worries with people who will listen and support them in any way they can. We are right by your side every step of the way

Academic Support

There are many services available here at the university for students to gain support for whatever academic issues they may face. We have resources to help you along with your studies if you need any support with a particular module or exam help. We are here for you

Additional Study Needs Support

Our services are here to support those students that need additional study support with resources catering to all student needs.

Financial Support

We have resources available here to support students financially through bursaries,grants and more. No student should have to worry about funding for their own education so we help to take the worry away.

Careers Support

Unsure what path to take after university? We are here to support you through the many paths available to you such as further postgraduate studies. We are also here to support you in getting into placement opportunities, helping you improve your CV in order to help you get into the career you want
